Aysenlur: Genesis

Between spring 2016 and autumn 2017, recording in Blackflag Studio their sixth album called "Away From Our Lines", where they back to their music origins, with distorted and Avant Garde harmonies, along with an extraordinary vocal strength. In December of the same year album was released and consolidating its cooperation with record label, BlackFlag Records.
