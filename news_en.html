AYSENLUR IN RUSSIAN COMPILATORY ALBUM.

March 12, 2018

The Chilean band has the privilege of being part of "Last Prophecy Compilation 2018", an album of Russian origin that brings together bands from all over the world and from different genres of metal.

"Injunction" is the song with which the band AysenluR participates in this musical compilation, managing to show part of their new work in other continents and share with bands from all over the world.

Download this interesting compilation of bands now!

AYSENLUR: NEW DISC OUT NOW.

February 19, 2018

AWAY FROM OUR LINES is the title of the new record label released by the Chilean group AYSENLUR; work that returns to the origins that characterized the band with distorted and avant- garde harmonies and the colossal strength in the voice, which without a doubt will not leave indifferent to those who listen to them.

Once again under the eaves of Blackflag Records, and this time with the musical production of Bojan Ostojic, this production has 5 cuts of a melodic Avant-Garde Black Metal, as they have called the genre, showing a marked game on the strings and neat work on the drums.
